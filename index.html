    Deals 110% DMG (+250) to the target and restore 10~20 energy.

    Rain of Fire - 30


    Deals 150% DMG (+450) to the target and makes it receive 30% more DMG for any subsequent direct attack.

    Thunderstorm - 60


    Deals 300% DMG (+1150) to the target. Also, increases the amount of healing by 50% for the next heal skill.

    Bane of Death - 40


    Deals 150% DMG (+150) and another 120% DMG to the target every turn for 3 turns.


  • Elemental Mage is an excellent choice if you want a balanced gameplay. This class possesses a range of basic elemental abilities, yet very powerful if played right. Recommended for beginner.





  • Arrow of Light - 0


    Deals 100% DMG (+200) to the target. Marks it with a Hunter's Mark for 1 turn. Restores 10~20 energy.

    Bloodshed - 30


    Deals 200% DMG (+470) to the target and another 125% DMG to the target every turn for 3 turns.

    Piercing Shot - 60


    Deals 380% DMG (+1050) to the target. If the target is under Hunter's Mark, deals an additional amount of 150% DMG.

    Hunter's Instinct - 40


    Increases Critical DMG by 60% and Critical Rate by 30% for 2 turns and mark the enemy with Hunter's Mark.


  • Forest Archer's gameplay is one of the riskiest. You lose more to gain more, they once said. This class includes many powerful stat boosts and abilities that are sure to hit the enemy like a truck. A glass truck, though. Recommended for anyone who enjoys living a dangerous life.





  • Righteousness - 0


    Deals 120% DMG (+240) to the target and decreases its ATK by 20% for 2 turns and mark it with a Light mark. Restores 10~20 energy.

    Rectitude - 30


    Deals 140% DMG (+300) to the target and marks it with a Light mark. Also, increases self DEF by 30% for 2 turns.

    Judgment - 60


    Deals 330% DMG (+1315) to the target. For each Light mark on the target, deals an additional 25% of DEF as DMG and removes all Light marks.

    Honor - 40


    Deals 160% DMG (+500) and another 12% of Max HP as DMG if more than 5 Light Marks are present. After use, removes 2 Light Marks. After that, grants a shield of 50% of his Max HP


  • Paladin of Light is one of those classes which seem casual but actually very strategic. The more wisely you use his skills, the faster you get to end the fight. This class comes with minimum offensive stats while having abilities that can send you to the extreme. Recommended for people who prefer strategy over power.





  • Soul Siphon - 0


    Deals 110% DMG (+280) to the target and marks it with a Soul Siphon mark. Restores 10 energy.

    Mind Gleaning - 30


    Deals 180% DMG (+700) to the target. If the target is under Soul Siphon mark, restores HP by 50% of DMG dealt. Places a Mind Gleaning mark on the enemy.

    Painless Death - 80


    Deals 380% DMG (+1500) to the target. If:
    -the target is under Soul Siphon, deals an additional 90% DMG and restores HP by 70% of DMG dealt. Then, removes the mark.
    -the target is under Mind Gleaning, deals an additional 80% DMG and restores 50 energy. Then, removes the mark.
    -the target is under both marks, deals an additional 200% DMG and restores both HP and energy to full. Then, removes all marks.

    Song of Moonlight - 40


    Deals 150% DMG (+600) to the target. If it has any mark, gains a Moon buff for 2 turns to increase DMG by 30%.


  • Take one to know one. That's what they said. You take my blood, I'll take your life. The Necromancer boasts self healing skills and marking abilities that will drive your gameplay strategy to its best. Recommended for hardcore player.





  • Blood Embrace - 0


    Deals 120% DMG (+310) to the target. If his HP is less than 50%, gain 1 Blood Sigil. Restores 15 Energy.

    Rosemary's Gift - 30


    Deals 160% DMG (+670) to the target. Deals an additional amount of 100% of his loss HP as DMG. Gain 1 Blood Sigil.

    Ichor Retaliation - 80


    Deals 410% DMG (+1800) to the target. For each Blood Sigil, deals an additional of 50% DMG and 3% of Max HP. Removes all Blood Sigils after that. For each removed Blood Sigil, grants a shield of 15% of his Max HP.

    Crimson Vitality - 40


    Increases his Max HP by 50% for 3 turns. Also, grants a shield of 30% of his loss HP.


  • The Knight of Blood gets strongest when his health is at his lowest. Challenge the extremity with this class to prove yourself a hardcore strategist.
